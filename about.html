Our Story

Locally Owned, Community Loved, and Built on Customization.

The U Top It Difference

"The ultimate destination for your custom cravings. Whether you're topping your perfect frozen treat or enjoying an authentic deli favorite, **U Top It** offers quality, variety, and fun in every bite."

Established in Grand Junction

U Top It was founded with a simple idea: to give the customer total control over their experience. Starting primarily as a self-serve frozen yogurt shop, we quickly became a favorite spot for families and students alike looking for a fun, personalized dessert.

As our community grew, so did our vision. We expanded our menu to include unique, high-quality savory items, adding authentic **Mexican Tortas** and delicious **Italian Paninis** to make us a true all-day destination.

Our Commitment to You

  • Freshness First: We use fresh ingredients, from our fruit toppings to our deli meats and bread.

  • Family Friendly: We provide a clean, vibrant, and welcoming atmosphere for all ages.

  • Local Love: As a local Grand Junction business, we are committed to supporting our neighbors and serving our community with a smile.
